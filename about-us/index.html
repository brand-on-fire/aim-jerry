Company Profile

NINGBO AIMJERRY is a dynamic, fast-growing provider of fashionable and functional household and personal-use goods to countries around the world. Working in close collaboration with only top sub-suppliers and independent designers, and keeping our “finger on the pulse” of market trends, we consistently deliver high-quality and the latest designs, with excellent service yet lower pricing than our best competition. Whether it be an area rug, floor mat, shower curtain, travel pillow, or other useful item we give you something that looks fantastic, is highly practical for a long time, and often up to 15% less expensive! What’s more, all AIMJERRY products are guaranteed non-toxic and environmentally friendly.

Plainly stated, AIMJERRY does things differently - precisely the way Founder and President Jerry Shen has intended. We’re a subsidiary of a much larger company ($75 million USD) whose vast resources and experience we share, yet run with all the passion and new ideas of a start-up. We don’t have more production and design partners - we have better partners with whom we share profits. We know that to keep customers for the long run we must deliver to them value, and solve their everyday problems. Ensuring that all stakeholders actually hold a stake in that success is a winning formula for us day in and day out. In that way, our passion translates into the creative, innovative, perfected designs that have our name on the label.

Moreover, we at AIMJERRY create that value - top design at lower cost - on a daily basis by improving daily. “Continuous improvement” is supposedly a mantra for many companies, though few actually put it into practice. Individually and as a company, with processes, communication, and offerings we are objectively reflective while actively seeking feedback from all quarters. In the office or on the factory floor, in QC or Shipping, we take a critical eye not just towards the products, but towards ourselves. Customers around the globe agree, and we know you will too.

Brand Story

Often before you do, NINGBO AIMJERRY knows what household and everyday use products you want, and how you want them. We have a passion for creating functional art, and making it artfully functional. It shows not only in the designs themselves and the standard with which we bring them to life, but in pricing that directly results from a constant strive for excellence. For even remotely comparable quality, our prices are often 15% cheaper for items such as area rugs, floor mats, shower curtains, and travel pillows.

In many ways the story of AIMJERRY, as both a company and a brand, parallels the ideas, philosophies, and personality of founder and GM Jerry Shen. It also aligns with his professional story and business aims - hence the company and brand name. With many years’ experience in sourcing and sales at OUNAN, AIMJERRY’s parent company, Jerry had the notion to leverage OUNAN’S vast resources and experience, take advantage of the nimbleness and focus a start-up company would have, and work with top independent designers around the globe. With that concept in mind and with the blessing of OUNAN’S progressive and flexible executive management, AIMJERRY was established as a subsidiary.

Do unto others as you would have done unto you” is a rule of thumb worldwide regardless of culture. So why should providing consumer products be any different? At AIMJERRY, we think it shouldn’t be. Jerry set out to provide households the world over the same kinds of safe, useful, long-lasting, affordable products he and his wife wanted in their home, to make environments like they make for their 3-year old daughter. Not only set out on that mission, but succeeded and continues to succeed in those lofty aims month after month, as new designs roll in, and new products roll out.

Those designs come in not only from China, Japan and other Asian locations but also from such diverse places as Denmark, the US, and Russia. Although AIMJERRY owns the IP rights to all designs, we actively engage in profit-sharing with designers - just like we do with the fine manufacturing partners we have in an equally varied number of places. Consistently great design and high-quality are hard enough to achieve on their own, let alone leading the market for pricing. It is precisely this business model, though, that makes that happen. If you take care of someone else, they’ll take care of you. The way it should be in both life and business, right? That, in essence, is the AIMJERRY story - thinking of everyone as family. Your home is your castle, but we help you furnish it, beautify it, and guard it. Home is where the heart is. AimJerry - “our designs go straight to the heart”
